FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take to set up a Shopify store?+
A standard store setup typically takes 3–7 days depending on complexity, number of products, and specific customizations required. I always provide a clear timeline before we begin.
Do I need a Shopify subscription before hiring you?+
Yes, you'll need an active Shopify account. I can guide you through the signup process and help you select the right plan for your business needs and budget.
Can you redesign my existing Shopify store?+
Absolutely — store redesigns are one of my specialties. I'll review your current store, understand your brand goals, and transform it into a professional, conversion-focused storefront.
What niches do you specialize in?+
I've worked across fashion & apparel, kitchen & home, beauty & skincare, electronics, and footwear. I can adapt to any niche and create a store perfectly suited to your target market.
Do you offer revisions after the project is complete?+
Yes — revisions are built into my process. We review the work together before final delivery and I make adjustments until you're completely satisfied with the result.
How do we communicate during the project?+
We can communicate via WhatsApp, email, or Fiverr — whichever you prefer. I provide regular updates throughout the project and am always responsive to your questions.
Do you handle marketing after the store is set up?+
Yes — I offer e-commerce marketing services including social media marketing, product promotion, and SEO to drive consistent traffic and sales to your store.
